04 / 09
AI-powered
From vague goal
to actionable plan.
Describe a task — Boardtivity's AI breaks it into timed subtasks automatically, with realistic time estimates for each step.
01 — Breakdown

"Ship the landing page redesign"

AI generates: Write copy (20 min) → Design mockup (45 min) → Build in code (60 min) → QA + deploy (20 min). Total: 145 min. Editable and re-generatable.

02 — Time linking

Subtasks drive the total

Adjust any subtask's time estimate and the total updates automatically. No more guessing how long something will take — build from the bottom up.

03 — Focus flow

One subtask at a time

After breakdown, hit Start Focus. The timer walks you through each subtask sequentially, showing what's next and keeping you locked in until it's done.

04 — No AI overhead

It disappears when not needed

AI features are opt-in. Skip breakdown entirely and Boardtivity works as a fast, clean task and idea board — no AI clutter in the way.
