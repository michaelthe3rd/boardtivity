## What We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign up, we collect your **email address** and basic profile info (like your name, if you provide it). Authentication is handled by **Clerk**, so we never see or store your password directly.

If you sign in with Google, Clerk receives your Google profile info on our behalf. We only use what's needed to identify your account.

### Board Content
Everything you create in Boardtivity — boards, task cards, idea cards, thought cards — is stored in our database. We store this so we can show it back to you. We don't read your content, sell it, or use it to train AI models.

### Waitlist Emails
If you sign up for the waitlist before creating an account, we store your email address to notify you when features or plans become available.

### Session Analytics
We track anonymous session data to understand how people use the app. This includes:
- A randomly generated **session ID** stored in your browser's `sessionStorage` (not a cookie — it clears when you close the tab)
- **Heartbeat events** sent every 30 seconds while you're active, so we can estimate time spent on site
- Basic browser and device info (e.g., screen size, browser type)

This data is anonymous. It is not linked to your account unless you're signed in, and even then, it's used only in aggregate to improve the product.

### Usage and Log Data
Our hosting provider (Vercel) and backend (Convex) may log standard server-side data like IP addresses, request timestamps, and response codes. This is standard infrastructure logging and is not used to profile individual users.

## Cookies and Local Storage

We use **sessionStorage** (not cookies) to store your anonymous session ID. sessionStorage is cleared automatically when you close your browser tab — it does not persist between sessions.

We may use minimal cookies through Clerk for authentication purposes (keeping you signed in). These are strictly functional and not used for advertising or tracking.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.
